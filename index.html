LANDER

After David Braben (Acorn Archimedes, 1987)

Click to play. Esc to release the cursor.Tap to dismiss. Use the stick + buttons.

  • Mouse — tilt the ship
  • Left button / Space — thrust
  • Middle button / H / Shift — hover
  • Right button / F — fire (−1 pt)
  • Stick — tilt the ship
  • THRUST — engines on
  • HOVER — auto-balance
  • FIRE — shoot (−1 pt)

Land level on the grey pad to refuel. Shoot trees, buildings and gazebos for score. Watch for falling rocks once you pass 800.
